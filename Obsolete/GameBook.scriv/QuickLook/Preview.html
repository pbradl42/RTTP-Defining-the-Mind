    History of classifications of mental illness

    While behaviors associated with mental illness—hallucinations, catatonic states, mania, etc.—have been known since ancient times, psychiatry dates its regularization as a scientific discipline to the work of the French physician Phillipe Pinel. His Traité médico-philosophique sur l'aliénation mentale; ou la manie stands as the first attempt at a scientific classification of mental illness.
